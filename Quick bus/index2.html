Rules and regulations to be followed when travelling.

  1. Reporting time is at least 30 minutes before departure.
  2. Any passenger left for late arrival shall not be allowed to the ticket.
  3. Passagers are advised to carry luggage weighing less than 30kgs. If otherwise are advised to board it on our cargo carriers.
  4. Only children under five years will be allowed to travel free only if he/she is accompanied by adult
  5. Each passenger is only allowed to carry one hand bag that can fit in the bus carrier.
  6. No luggage will be carried in the corridor or under the seats.
